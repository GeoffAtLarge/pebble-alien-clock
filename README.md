# pebble-alien-clock
A watchface for the Pebble Time 2 based on a Star Trek Deep Space 9 working prop called The Alien Clock

The original clock was manufactured by Alien Time Company in Newhall, CA. I was fortunate enough to purchase one of these new and it's my favorite desk clock. This watchface is an homage to that clock and I do not own the rights to the visual design or clock display logic.

Learning to read the time is fairly simple but it still takes a second or two for my brain to figure it out. This ensures that I only know what time it is when I really want to know.

## Telling time with The Alien Clock
- Hours are displayed on the horizontal row of lights in the middle of the face
  - Green lights represent single hours
  - Red lights represent 5 hours
- Minutes are displayed in the ring of lights around the hours and in the vertical row of lights in the center of the face
  - Green lights represent single minutes
  - The single red light represents 5 minutes
  - Yellow lights represent 10 minutes
- Seconds are displayed in the horizontal row of lights at the bottom of the face
  - Seconds increment using binary addition (1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32)

 ## Added features for Pebble watches
 - You can turn on or off the labels to remind you what the lights mean
 - You can completely turn off the seconds to conserve battery life
 - You can change the colors of the lights
